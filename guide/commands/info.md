# Info

Gives you information about the bot such as its uptime and the bot owner.

## Usage

<br />
<DiscordMessages>
	<DiscordMessage profile="user">
		/info
	</DiscordMessage>
	<DiscordMessage profile="bot">
		Owner: Link#0069<br />
        Uptime: 2 days ago<br />
        Ping: 256ms<br />
		Contributors: Sawa Kyouki#3622<br />
	</DiscordMessage>
</DiscordMessages>
