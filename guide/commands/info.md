# Info

Gives you information about the bot such as its uptime and the bot owner.

## Usage
<br />
<DiscordMessages>
	<DiscordMessage profile="user">
		/info
	</DiscordMessage>
	<DiscordMessage profile="bot">
		Bot Owner: Link#0069
        Bot Version: 2.0.0
        Bot Uptime: 2 days
        Bot Ping: 108ms
	</DiscordMessage>
</DiscordMessages>
